Basic beef broth from bones

Active Time:

Total Time:

Serves:

← Back to

Ingredients

  • Beef bones
  • Onion
  • Carrot
  • Water
  • Bay leaf
  • Oil
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Water

Directions

  1. Heat oven to 375-400 F
  2. Drizzle beef bones with a bit of oil, and spread them out on a baking sheet
  3. Roughly chop onion and carrot, drizzle with oil, and add to baking sheet
  4. Salt and pepper, if desired
  5. Bake the bones and veggies for half hour, ensuring bones brown. Flip bones over and bake until both sides are browned.
  6. Once bones are browned, add bones and veggies to large pot. Cover with water. Add apple cider vinegar (lemon juice or tomato paste will also work). Allow to simmer on low all day, or overnight.
  7. Skim fat from top periodically (if desired), and keep adding water so the bones are covered.
  8. Once it's simmered long enough, remove bones and veggie chunks. Strain the broth so it's smooth
  9. Store broth in fridge or freezer
  10. Use for cooking, or can be consumed as broth soup. Add seasoning as needed when preparing a meal.
